Alexczar E. Dela Torre

Complex Systems Researcher

I am a Research Assistant at De La Salle University (DLSU) in the Philippines. I am part of the System for Optimized Routing for Transport (SORT) project which is funded by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST). In this project, we create and maintain simulation models of the three rapid transit train systems (LRT-1, LRT-2, and MRT-3) in Metro Manila through a technique called agent-based modeling. We observe these models to gain emergent insights on the capital region's train lines as brought about by the interplay between the passenger crowds and the trains deployed. In the future, we plan on expanding our simulations to other modes of transport.
